# Background Texture Request — Cream Paper/Linen

## Purpose
A subtle, seamless repeating texture to use as a section background on the More Tea Community website. Adds warmth and tactile feel without being distracting.

## Description
A very light, barely-there paper or linen texture. Think: aged cream stationery, or a fine linen tablecloth. The texture should be almost flat — just enough grain/weave to feel organic rather than like a plain flat color.

## Specs
- Size: 600 x 600px (it will tile seamlessly)
- Base color: warm cream/off-white — something like #f5efe6 or #f0e8d8
- Texture intensity: very subtle, roughly 10–15% opacity grain over the base color
- Format: PNG
- Must tile seamlessly (no visible edge when repeated)
- NO pattern, NO lines, NO decorative elements — pure texture only

## Tone
Matches the warm brown palette of the More Tea Community logo (cream background, dark brown tones).
