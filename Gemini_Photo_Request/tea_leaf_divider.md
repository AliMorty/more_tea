# Decorative Divider Request — Tea Leaf / Steam Element

## Purpose
A small horizontal decorative element to use as a visual divider between sections on the More Tea Community website.

## Description
A simple, elegant horizontal illustration: a few loose tea leaves scattered gently left to right, or a delicate wisp of steam curling upward from the center. The style should feel hand-drawn or lightly illustrated — not overly detailed.

## Specs
- Canvas size: 400 x 60px
- Background: fully transparent (PNG with alpha)
- Color: warm brown — approximately #7a4f2e or #c8a97e (to match the logo palette)
- Style: minimal line illustration or watercolor sketch — not photographic
- The element should be centered and leave empty space on both sides so it works as a section break

## Examples of acceptable styles
- 3–5 scattered tea leaves in a loose horizontal arrangement
- A single gentle steam curl rising from the center
- A simple horizontal line with a small tea leaf or teacup icon at the center

## What to avoid
- Anything too ornate or Victorian
- Drop shadows or 3D effects
- Text or letterforms
