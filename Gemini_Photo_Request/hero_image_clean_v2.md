Wide hero banner image for the More Tea Community website homepage. No text should appear in the image — text will be added via HTML/CSS.

A wide, cinematic-style photograph of a single glass tea mug filled with warm amber tea, sitting on a dark wooden table. Soft steam rising from the top. Warm, moody lighting — like late afternoon light coming through a window. The background is slightly blurred (bokeh) with hints of a cozy room: perhaps books, a plant, or soft fabric in the background.

The overall mood is: warm, inviting, intellectual, community-oriented.

- Aspect ratio: approximately 16:5 (very wide, short) — like 1600 x 500px
- Style: realistic photographic, not illustrated
- Colors: warm amber, deep brown, cream — no cool blues or grays
- NO text, NO logos, NO watermarks in the image
